Wire Choir 2.0 is an audition based group with-in Wire Choir. This section of Wire choir is meant for students who want more of a challenge. This section of Wire choir is made up of existing members who auditioned to join. if chosen to join they are then part of both Wire Choir and Wire Choir 2.0.

2.0 is the section of wire choir that plays more of the performences that Wire Choir is requested to play at. These are the performances that can't adccomadate the entire group and so then 2.0 is selected to play. they are also the group that will play some of the more challenging music that wire choir has to offer.
