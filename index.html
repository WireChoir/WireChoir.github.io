Music Forms Contacts

Wire Choir was created by Elissa Jones Maynard in 2005. Elissa is a professional Violinist, Music Educator, and Singer-Songwriter from the Hudson Valley NY. Initially the club began with 15 students and throughout the years grew in popularity to 100 students.

Wire Choir is student centered allowing students who have been in the group a long time to have leadership positions, allowing them to be Co-Leaders and Officers of the group such as, President, Vice President, Secretary, Treasurer, Class Representative, Choreographer, Technology Specialist, Design Artist, and Librarian. these oppurtunities give the students a chance to be a leader and gain expierence in leading the young and setting a good example. The Wire Choir has performed in Colorado Springs at various venues, Hyde Park, Music Festivals, Veteran's Day Performances, Holiday Celebrations and more.

Wire Choir is a non-traditional pops orchestra which focuses on learning techniques through popular music. Students in Wire Choir not only learn techniques, but they learn how to stroll and perform for a live audience with enthusiasm. Students also learn the ability to memorize music which is a necessity for the group.

Wire Choir is a club at the Warwick Valley Central School district in Warwick NY. It is available to all students from 5th - 12th grade and although it says choir in the name this is an orchesta group.
